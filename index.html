ConnectingIT Technologies:-

ConnectingIT Technologies is an end to end IT training solutions. The data is staggering, millions of unemployed, skilled graduates, several thousand engineering, and job vacancies do not match upto the figures, and every year there is a unemployment backlog, people who did not get a job on the year of passing and now have to compete with the next year flow of passouts. Not to mention the huge Academic-Corporate exepectation GAP, skill set MISMATCH. With this abysmal state of affairs, we started as a one stop shop for freshers to become more employable, by learning w at Industry requires, bridging the expectation GAP and finally leveraging on our strengths to get them suitably placed.


Mission
Make every technical graduate employable and promote Digital India with diverse skill sets.

Vision
Our immediate mission is to ensure we train atleast 500+ graduates yearly in Delhi NCR and make them employable.

The Problem

  • Lakhs of IT Graduates passing out- BTech, BE, BScIT, MScIT.

  • Limited job opportunities-1 many applicants preference given to Tier 1-IIT / NIT.

  • Campus placements happen only for reputed colleges.

  • The backlog syndrome - this year 20000 passouts 5000 jobs backlog.

  • Next year again 22000 Pass outs, 7000 jobs, backlog previous year 15000+ this year.

  • And this keeps adding up, so if you are not employed by year 2 you have missed the bus.
